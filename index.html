TREASURE

TREASURE is a 10-member boy group under YG Entertainment through the survival show YG Treasure Box. The group consists of: Choi Hyunsuk, Park Jihoon, Yoshi, Kim Junkyu, Yoon Jaehyuk, Asahi, Haruto, Kim Doyoung, Park Jeongwoo, and So Junghwan. They used to to be 12-members which is Mashiho and Bang Yedam officially departed Treasure and terminated their contract with YG Entertainment on November 8, 2022, to focus on health and producing, respectively. They debuted on August 7, 2020, with their first single album, The First Step: Chapter One with the song called Boy. They also had their fandom name called Treasure Maker but their leader, Choi Hyunsuk decided to create a short name since it is long words to call which is "Teume". As of January 1, 2025, TREASURE changed its leadership structure, with members Asahi and Junkyu taking over as the new leaders from departing leaders Hyunsuk and Jihoon. This change is part of a new YG Entertainment strategy to rotate leadership every 2-3 years to foster growth, encourage a more diverse sound, and empower different members.
