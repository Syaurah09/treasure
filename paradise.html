Love Pulse is the third mini-album by TREASURE, released on September 1, 2025. The album is praised for its modern groove and rich production, blending TREASURE's signature high energy with more experimental sounds.

The title track of this album is PARADISE. The genre was disco-funk. This song was produced by P.K and Choice37 (long-time YG veterans) collaborated to give it that "stadium" feel while keeping it refined and sensuous. It captures the "fluttering" excitement of a first love. Next, the second song of this album, EVERYTHING. The vibe was it features a bright, refreshing synth sound. DEE.P, who has worked on many of TREASURE's most melodic hits, led the production here to ensure the song had a high-energy, "fresh" opening impact.
