THE FIRST STEP:
CHAPTER THREE

Songs:

  • MMM
  • ORANGE

The First Step: Chapter Three is the third single album by Treasure. The single album was released for pre-order on 27 October. The single album was produced by Aftrshok, Czaer and Future Bounce. The First Step: Chapter Three is primarily a rap & hiphop record with lo-fi and trap elements.

On October 29, the track list for the album was revealed. The album will contain a total of four tracks, including two new songs and their instrumental versions. Members Yoshi, Choi Hyunsuk and Haruto were credited as writers, while Asahi was credited as not only the writer but also as a composer for the first time. Not long after that, on November 2, the group revealed 18 seconds of the dance clip of the lead single "Mmm" five times every midnight, revealing half the song, which is approximately 1 minute and 30 seconds, prior to the release of the album.

According to YG Entertainment, “MMM” is a song in typical hiphop genre with a BPM of 80, which is slower than the previous releases, but it will be a song sending off a stronger impression. On the other hand, "Orange" is a ballad B-side track from this album describes a couple who want to spend more time together but are forced to say goodbye as the sky turns orange. Basically it’s describe about focusing on the regret of parting with a loved one at sunset. This song was composed and written by their member Asahi.
